National Pyjama Day

February 9, 2018

Sunbeam Creche is proudly participating in the 15th annual National Pyjama Day taking place on March 9. This year's choosen disability is Autism. Children and staff are sponsored to wear their pyjamas for the day, the day will include activities such as storytelling, teddy bears picnic, singing songs and a little party.

We ask all parents who wish for their child to take part to sign up below for their child's sponsorship package. Each sponsorship package includes a sponsor envelope, stickers and balloons. We would like to take this opportunity to thank everyone who is getting involved this year as this event is for an excellent cause.
