Our Staff

Here at Sunbeam Creche our staff are highly qualified and have years of experience between them. They are very friendly, caring and have a love for children.

Tracey Greene

Tracey has 10 years of experience in childcare, she is a very kind-hearted women and loves working with children. Tracey has many qualifications. As well as being a fully qualified Special Needs Assistant she has her Childcare Level 6, Speech & Language for preschool children, Child protection, Aistear, Manual handling, First Aid and a Full Irish drivers licence.

Tracey is the first face of contact here at Sunbeams Creche. As owner of the creche Tracey has many roles but still works in our Early years room with our babies and toddlers, she is a very kind person and loves to be around them daily. Tracey has a lot of time for all the children in the facility and is like the "Mammy" of the Creche.
